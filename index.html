Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications developed and published by BrainFreeze Studios, including Sudoku Quest and Rico Rico.

1. Information We Collect

Our apps are designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to external servers. All data including your player name, game progress, settings, and coin balance is stored locally on your device only.

2. Data Storage

BrainFreeze Studios apps save everything locally on your device. This includes:

  • Player name and password (stored locally, never transmitted)
  • Game progress and statistics
  • App settings and preferences
  • In-app currency (coins) balance

3. Advertising

Our apps may display advertisements to support free access. Future versions may integrate third-party advertising networks. When implemented, these networks may collect anonymized usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We will update this policy accordingly.

4. Children's Privacy

BrainFreeze Studios apps are designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our apps do not require account registration with any external service.

5. Third-Party Services

Our apps do not currently integrate any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services. Any future integrations will be disclosed in an updated Privacy Policy.

6. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information is protected by your device's own security features. We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
